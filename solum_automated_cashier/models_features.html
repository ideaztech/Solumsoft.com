SAC PRO

Through Wall ATM

The SAC PRO is the most popular ATM for recycling depots in Alberta. This model sits behind a secure wall and only a portion of the machine is seen through a window style opening. It is very secure and comparable to a traditional ATM bank machine. All the maintenance and management of the machine is done from the back. It has a maximum cash capacity of $37,533.00.

 

New features include: Smart Card and QuickDrop compatible and new status monitoring methods.

 

Now Available in Stainless Steel.
